Zero Trust Networking Principles

  • Least Privilege Access: Network access restricted by role, VLAN, and routing policy
  • Network Segmentation: VLAN-based segmentation to isolate users, servers, and management networks
  • Controlled East–West Traffic: Inter-VLAN routing explicitly defined and monitored
  • Continuous Monitoring: Performance and availability monitored to detect anomalies
  • Assume Breach Mindset: Network design limits lateral movement and blast radius
